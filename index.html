Welcome

Thank you for visiting my academic website. I’m Debarshi Roy, currently an MTech (Research) Scholar at the Microbiome Informatics Lab, Department of Computational Biology, IIIT Delhi. My work lies at the intersection of biology, artificial intelligence, and large-scale data analysis. I am deeply interested in computational approaches to understand the human microbiome and its role in aging, disease, and precision medicine.

About Me

I have a strong background in Computer Science and Data Science, with research experience across IIIT Delhi, IIT Guwahati, and IISER Bhopal. My academic journey started with a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, and evolved toward computational biology through interdisciplinary research.

I work on microbiome data analysis, machine learning for biological systems, and AI-based predictive modeling for healthcare. My long-term goal is to pursue a Ph.D. in Computational Biology, focusing on scalable, data-driven pipelines for microbiome analytics and disease modeling.

Outside research, I mentor GATE aspirants, enjoy cricket, chess, swimming, drama, and reading non-fiction literature.
